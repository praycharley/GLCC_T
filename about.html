About Us

Our Story

Global Light Charismatic Chapel was founded on a vision to bring God's light to the nations. Through spirit-led worship, vibrant fellowship, and practical teachings, we are building a family of believers dedicated to shining for Christ in every area of life.

Our Mission

To empower individuals with the love of Jesus Christ, equip them with the Word of God, and send them to impact their communities and the world.

Our Core Values

Faith

We walk by faith, not by sight, trusting fully in God's promises.

Love

We believe love is the greatest commandment and our highest calling.

Service

We serve God and our community with humility and excellence.

Our Leadership

Led by Spirit-filled leaders, our pastors and ministry team are passionate about shepherding God's people and equipping the saints for works of service.
